What does it do?

This tool is for comparing Steam accounts and their game libraries. It was mainly made for Steam family, seeing which member contributed most in the family, but it can also be used to compare with the Steam accounts of random people or with friends

How to use it

  1. Enter how many accounts to compare
  2. Input their Steam IDs (these can be found in the links of their accounts)
  3. On the same page of inputting Steam IDs, choose what to compare and optional filters, then submit.
  4. Done! You can also choose what type of graph to display the data
